About Us

In 2014, the initial company named CV. Muara Jaya Transindo was officially established with a main focus in freight forwarding and customs document processing services.

In the journey, CV. Muara Jaya Transindo has succeeded in becoming one of the companies trusted by various companies to provide logistics services and customs documents for their industrial activities.

With the spirit of becoming a leading company, for image and brand development, the founder of CV. Muara Jaya Transindo in July 2019 agreed to set up another company and form a group called Great Group Indonesia. The companies that are members of the Great Group Indonesia are:

1. CV. Muara Jaya Transindo
     Vessel (Liquid/Dry) Chartering, FCL & LCL Services, Land Transport and Custom Clearance Services

2. PT. Great Inti Dinamis
     A trading company for various agricultural products

3. PT. Great Food Industri
     Industrial company engaged in the food industry, essential oils and spices
